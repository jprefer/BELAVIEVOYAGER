What we offer


Pre-Conference Preparation

   Planning and Logistics: Define the conference objectives, themes, and agenda. Choose a venue – negotiate a favorable contract ensuring best pricing and perks, choose the AV company that best suits your needs and budget – whether in-house or outsourced, set dates, and determine the budget. Arrange for accommodations, catering, entertainment and technical requirements.
    Speakers and Content : Coordinate speaker schedules and communications. Collect presentation materials in advance.
    Registration and sales: Set up an online registration system and manage ticket sales. Set up promo codes, e.g. speakers, sponsors, Board of Directors, if needed.
    Networking Opportunities: Plan networking sessions, receptions, exhibitor booths, and social events to encourage interaction among attendees, sponsors, and speakers.

On-site Management

   Oversee the setup of the venue, registration desks, signage, decor, and technical equipment. Ensure everything runs smoothly and troubleshoot any issues that arise.
    Backend Coordination - Oversee check-ins, catering, audio visual, and rooms.
    Attendee Experience - Provide support to attendees, answer questions, and address concerns. Collect feedback through surveys or comment cards.
    Vendor and Sponsor Management - Coordinate with sponsors and exhibitors, ensuring they have what they need and that their contributions align with the conference's goals.

Post-Conference

   Follow-Up : Send thank-you notes to speakers, sponsors, and attendees. Share presentations or resources from the conference. Gather and analyze feedback to improve future events.
    Financial Review: Reconcile expenses, review budget adherence, and generate financial reports to assess the conference's success and areas for improvement.
    Documentation and Evaluation: Document key insights, successes, challenges, and lessons learned. Conduct a comprehensive evaluation to gauge attendee satisfaction and identify areas for enhancement.
    Vendor and Sponsor Management - Coordinate with sponsors and exhibitors, ensuring they have what they need and that their contributions align with the conference's goals.
